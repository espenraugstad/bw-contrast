Contrast on Black and White

This page is based on code written by Benze Szabo and explained in detail in the article Which Colors Look Good on Black and White?

Choose a contrast level to see colors that have this contrast or above on both black and white.

Granularity determines how much the r, g, and b values should increase for each color. A low granularity calculates many colors, and MAY CRASH THE SITE for low contrast values. Choose wisely!

NOTE! WCAG recommends a minimum contrast of 4.5 for regular text on background.
